Our Philosophy

Perfumery as
Living Art

AURUM NOIR was born from a singular obsession — to create fragrances that transcend the ordinary and speak to the deepest layers of memory and desire.

Our master perfumer, trained for twelve years in Grasse, sources only the rarest botanical extracts — Bulgarian rose absolute, Haitian vetiver, Mysore sandalwood — blended by hand in quantities so small each batch is virtually unique.

How We Create

The Craft

01
🌿

Wild Harvest

Our botanists travel to remote regions to harvest flowers, roots and resins at the exact moment of peak aromatic potential.

02
⚗️

Cold Extraction

Each ingredient undergoes slow, cold-process extraction to preserve volatile compounds lost in conventional distillation.

03
🧪

Master Blending

Our perfumer hand-blends each accord over weeks, balancing notes across up to seven olfactory layers.

04
✦

Aging & Sealing

The final blend rests for 90 days in sealed darkness before hand-filling each numbered, wax-sealed bottle.
