The Story +

Noir Absolu was born from a vision of absolute darkness — not the void, but the rich, inhabited darkness of a forest floor at midnight. Our master perfumer spent three years perfecting this singular composition, building layer upon layer of smoky, resinous depth around a heart of ultra-premium Agarwood sourced from 80-year-old trees in Assam. The result is a fragrance that breathes, evolves, and ultimately becomes part of the wearer's own skin.

Ingredients & Sourcing +
  • Grade-A Agarwood (Oud) — sustainably harvested, Assam, India
  • Black Amber Absolute — cold-extracted in Grasse atelier
  • Mysore Sandalwood — ethically sourced, CITES certified
  • Labdanum Absolute — wild-harvested, Crete, Greece
  • All ingredients are natural; no synthetic materials
How to Wear +

Apply 2–3 sprays to pulse points — the inner wrist, neck, and behind the ears. The warmth of your skin will amplify the deeper notes. Noir Absolu develops over 6–8 hours; allow it time to bloom before forming judgements. It performs exceptionally on clean, moisturised skin.

Packaging & Delivery +

Each bottle is individually filled by hand, wax-sealed, and numbered. It arrives in a hand-stitched noir lacquered box with a signed certificate of authenticity. Estimated delivery: 5–8 business days internationally, 2–3 days domestic.
